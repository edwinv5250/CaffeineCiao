Barista Tips

Start with fresh beans

Use within 2–4 weeks of roast date. Stale coffee is the top reason for flat, dull cups.

Bloom your coffee

Pour 2× coffee weight in water, wait 30 s. This releases CO₂ and unlocks even extraction.

Use filtered water

Chlorine dulls flavour. Filtered water around 150 ppm TDS is ideal for pour over.

Grind just before brewing

Ground coffee stales in minutes. A burr grinder dramatically improves clarity and sweetness.

Control your temperature

Light roasts: 92–95°C. Dark roasts: 88–91°C. Too hot = bitter. Too cool = sour.
